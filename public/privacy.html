Privacy Policy - SpareTime

Last updated: 2025-12-01

Data access

SpareTime accesses only the following Google API scope when you opt in to cloud backup:

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata

This scope grants SpareTime access to the AppData folder in your Google Drive. The AppData folder is a special, hidden storage area intended for application-specific files. SpareTime only reads and writes files that it creates in that AppData folder.

Why the data is accessed

When you enable backup, SpareTime stores and retrieves your app data (task list, settings, and sync metadata) in the user's AppData folder so your tasks can be restored if you switch devices or reinstall the app. This enables two-way sync and optional backups.

What is NOT accessed or shared

  • SpareTime does not access, list, or modify any of your other Drive files or folders.
  • SpareTime does not share your data with third parties.

Where the data is stored

Backup files are stored in your Google Drive AppData folder. These files are associated with your Google account and are private to you and the app.

How to revoke access

You can revoke SpareTime's access at any time from your Google Account:

  1. Open Google Account (https://myaccount.google.com)
  2. Go to Security → Third-party apps with account access (or Apps with access to your account)
  3. Find the SpareTime application and remove its access

Revoking access stops future backups and syncs. If you want to remove previously stored AppData, you can do so within the app's settings page or contact us for assistance.

Contact

For questions about privacy, contact: sparetime-support@googlegroups.com
